About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Video and Image Computing Lab (VIC Lab), KAIST, where I also received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering (Aug. 2025) under the supervision of Prof. Munchurl Kim. I currently lead an NRF/MSIT-funded Sejong Science Fellowship project on AI-based satellite image visualization and analysis.

My research centers on generative models for remote sensing — SAR-to-EO image translation, PAN-sharpening, and satellite image super-resolution — and on efficient skeleton-based action recognition. My current interests extend to remote sensing foundation models, skeleton foundation models, and motion generation.
